## 📖 Overview
**Kobi** (Bengali for "Poet") is a sanctuary for creative coding, experimental scripts, and the art of elegant programming. This repository explores the intersection of logic and creativity, showcasing scripts that are as beautiful in their implementation as they are in their output.

## ✨ Philosophy
- 🎨 **Code as Art**: Writing clean, readable, and aesthetic code.
- 🧪 **Experimental**: Testing new ideas and patterns.
- 🖋️ **Poetic Logic**: Finding the most elegant solutions to complex problems.

## 🚀 Contents
- Experimental Bash scripts.
- Visual algorithms.
- Small, focused utilities with a touch of creativity.
